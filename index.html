About Me

Welcome! My name is Christian. In 2025, I completed a PhD in Pure Mathematics at UNSW Sydney under the supervision of Prof. Igor Shparlinski and Dr. Bryce Kerr. My research focused on developing geometric and analytic techniques to tackle problems in number theory.

I now work as a Data Scientist at Audinate, where I enjoy working with data and seeing statistical techniques in action. I am also passionate about sports analytics, and I spend my free time building models to predict fantasy football outcomes.

Before that, I completed a degree in Pure Mathematics at the University of Calgary, where I was awarded a gold medal as the top student graduating with an honors degree in 2021.
