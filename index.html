About Me

Welcome! My name is Christian, and I have recently completed a PhD in Pure Mathematics at UNSW Sydney, working under the supervision of Prof. Igor Shparlinski and Dr. Bryce Kerr. My research focused on developing geometric and analytic techniques to tackle problems in number theory.

I am very interested in data science and machine learning, and I am currently enjoying working as a Data Scientist at Audinate here in Sydney.

I completed a degree in Pure Mathematics at the University of Calgary, where I was awarded a gold medal for being the top student in the university graduating with an honors degree in 2021.
